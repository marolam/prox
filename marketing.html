Grow the Prox network with us.

From day one, Prox will depend on real people sharing it in places where it can have the biggest impact. This page is the starting point for referral tools, marketing resources, and – later on – business-focused features.

Referral & growth roles.

We’re building a system where people who actively help grow Prox are recognized and rewarded. Think of it as a “field kit” for people who want to bring Prox into their city, niche, or community.

  • Unique referral links tied to your account.
  • Prox Points for verified new users and good meetups.
  • Optional “Prox promoter” or “community builder” badges.
  • Early access to new features in your region.

Business mode (future).

Down the road, Prox will include tools aimed at businesses, freelancers, and local professionals. Instead of shouting into the void, they’ll be able to connect with nearby people who are already looking for what they offer.

  • Business profiles tied to a physical location.
  • Availability modes – “here now,” “by appointment,” or “via Prox avatar.”
  • Prox Points and paid tools for responsible promotion, not spam.
