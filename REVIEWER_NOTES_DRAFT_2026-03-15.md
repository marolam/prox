## Account Deletion

### Public deletion URL
https://prox-us.com/delete-account

### In-app path
Settings → Account → Account controls → Delete account

### What gets deleted
- Account and profile information
- Authentication credentials
- Match history and in-app messages
- Preferences, settings, and feedback given or received

### What may be retained
- Legal and security records required by law or needed for fraud/abuse investigation
- Billing / transaction records required for tax or financial compliance (if applicable)
- Fully anonymised / aggregated data that can no longer identify the user

### Expected deletion timing
Deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation. Full purge from backup systems
may take up to 30 days.

### Support contact for deletion issues
support@prox-us.com
