Secure Your Spot
For everyday people

Use Prox to find who you’re looking for.

Prox is for people who want to move past endless feeds and actually connect. Whether you’re looking for help, skills, collaboration, or just a friendly meetup, Prox is built to get you face-to-face quickly and safely.

You control what you share.

Prox focuses on what matters: what you’re looking for and what you can provide. You choose your visible keywords, your matching radius, and when you’re discoverable. Location-based tools work only when you opt in.

  • Set your radius – from “right next door” to wider search.
  • Pick your keywords: “Can teach guitar,” “Need moving help,” “Looking for co-founder,” and more.
  • Toggle between personal mode, business mode (later), or off.
Examples of how people might use Prox
  • Someone who can fix bikes and someone whose chain just snapped.
  • A traveler looking for a local photographer.
  • Neighbors trading tools or help in a hurry.
  • People exploring new hobbies together, not just matching on interests in a bio.

What a typical meetup looks like.

  1. Open Prox and see who nearby matches what you need or offer.
  2. Start a chat with someone whose profile looks like a fit.
  3. Pick a public meetup spot using the map tools inside the app.
  4. Confirm arrival, meet up, and mark how it went afterwards.

Those ratings and confirmations feed back into the trust system, so over time reliable people naturally rise to the top.
