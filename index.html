Early access

Secure your spot as a Prox tester.

Before we invite the public, we’re opening a limited group of testers who will help shape how Prox works in the real world. You’ll get early builds, a direct line to share feedback, and a say in how meetups, trust, and Prox Points evolve.

At its core, Prox is about people actually meeting each other. As a tester, your notes and real-life experiences will help us make that process smoother, safer, and more enjoyable.

  • Get early access to the app.
  • Receive clear test instructions for each phase.
  • Submit feedback, issues, and ideas in one place.
  • Help us tune safety and trust features.

What we’re looking for
  • People who actually plan to meet up with others.
  • Honest feedback – what felt smooth, what didn’t.
  • Attention to safety and comfort in real-world use.
