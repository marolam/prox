Tester Portal

This is the central hub for Prox testers: access builds, see what to focus on, and send feedback after your sessions.

Welcome, Testers

Once you’ve been accepted into the tester program, this portal will contain:

  • Download links or store instructions for the latest test build
  • What’s new in this build (features to try, areas to stress-test)
  • Links to feedback forms and support
  • Known issues and any temporary workarounds

1. Get the Latest Build

Follow the instructions in your tester email to install the current version on your device. We’ll keep this space updated with release notes as we go.

2. Check the Focus Areas

Each round of testing will highlight certain flows: matching, chat, meetups, trust, or performance. Focus there first, then explore freely.

3. Send Feedback Quickly

Use the support form or in-app tools to send bugs, screenshots, and suggestions. Short, honest notes beat long essays.
