Uploads

Logs, screenshots, and videos.

When you run into something interesting (good or bad), capture it. Screenshots, short screen recordings, and logs are incredibly helpful when reproducing issues.

Use the button below to upload files (screenshots, videos, log files) to the shared folder.

Open upload folder

Tip: Include your name and date in filenames, e.g. marty-2025-12-04-login-bug.png.

What to upload
  • Crashes or freezes – screenshots or screen recordings.
  • Map / meetup issues – where things didn’t match reality.
  • Weird UI behavior – things that felt confusing or broken.
  • Positive surprises – when something felt especially smooth.
